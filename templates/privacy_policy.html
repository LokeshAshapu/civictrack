Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

CivicTrack ("we", "our", or "us") operates the CivicTrack website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you:

2.1 Personal Data

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Phone number
  • Cookies and Usage Data

2.2 Usage Data

We may also collect information on how the service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other diagnostic data.

3. Location Data

We request access to your location data to help you report civic issues at specific locations. This information is used to pinpoint problems on our map and help authorities identify and resolve issues more efficiently. You may enable or disable location services through your device settings.

4. Use of Data

CivicTrack uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our service
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our service
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

5. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Sharing of Information

Your personal information will be shared with government agencies and civic authorities only when necessary to resolve reported issues. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@civictrack.com
  • Website: www.civictrack.com

9. Data Retention

CivicTrack will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Personal data will be kept until the reported civic issue is resolved or deleted at user request.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
