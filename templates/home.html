About Us

CivicTrack is a citizen-driven platform designed to make cities more responsive, transparent, and efficient. It empowers individuals to actively participate in improving their surroundings by reporting everyday civic issues such as road damage, waste management problems, and public infrastructure faults.

At its core, CivicTrack believes that every voice matters. By providing a simple and accessible way to highlight local concerns, the platform encourages communities to take collective responsibility for their environment. Instead of issues going unnoticed, CivicTrack ensures they are visible, trackable, and prioritized based on real needs.
